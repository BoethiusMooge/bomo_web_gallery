The Dynamic Tree of Life

This interactive artwork represents the ever-changing journey of life. The branching structure of the tree symbolizes the various stages and decisions we make throughout our lives. By using Perlin noise to create a more organic look, the artwork aims to convey the complex and unpredictable nature of life's experiences.

Users are invited to engage with the piece by moving their mouse horizontally across the screen. The branching angle of the tree changes in response to the mouse position, illustrating how external influences can shape our lives. This interaction allows viewers to reflect on the interconnectedness of our choices and experiences, and the fluidity of life's journey.
