In this generative artwork, the artist invites us to explore the concept of identity and the way it can be perceived differently depending on the context. As the circles evolve in size, we are reminded of the ever-changing nature of our own selves representing the fluidity of our own identities, influenced by the different roles we play in life and the way we are perceived by others.

Through the process of deconstruction, the artwork breaks down the familiar into its fundamental components, prompting us to examine the essence of what makes us unique.
